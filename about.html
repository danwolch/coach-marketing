Meet the founder

Dan Wolchonok

VP of New Products at Reforge. 15+ years building products and growth systems at high-growth technology companies.

Background

Dan's career spans founding an acquired startup, five years at HubSpot driving product growth, and leadership roles at Reforge building the premier professional education platform for growth and product professionals.

After earning a Computer Science degree from Tufts University, Dan spent five years at Microsoft as a Technical Lead, working on enterprise search systems processing hundreds of millions of documents and millions of search queries per day.

In 2011, Dan enrolled at Yale School of Management. During his MBA, he founded PrepWork—a personal research assistant that was acquired by HubSpot in 2013, just nine months after founding.

That acquisition launched a five-year career at HubSpot, where Dan rose to Director of Product Analytics and helped grow Sidekick from 1,000 to over 300,000 weekly active users. Colleagues nicknamed him "Data Dan" for his relentless measurement and analysis approach.
