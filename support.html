Keep Your App Updated

For the best experience, make sure you're running the latest version of Work Coach. Updates include new features, performance improvements, and bug fixes.

Tip: Open the App Store on your device, search for "Work Coach," and tap Update if an update is available. You can also enable automatic updates in your device settings.

Common Questions

If you're still experiencing problems after updating, please email us with details about your issue, and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
