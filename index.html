Frequently asked questions

What do I need to get started?

Join the waitlist and tell us your goal. Once you're in, you download the Mac app and your coach starts learning from your meetings. Every plan begins with a 2-week free trial — no credit card required.

Is there a real human coach?

Yes — that's your choice. The base plan ($100/mo) is your always-on AI coach. Add human review of your progress ($250/mo), or a live monthly session with a real work coach ($500/mo). You can change tiers anytime.

Does a bot join my calls?

No. Work Coach captures audio locally on your Mac. No bot participant, no awkward notification. No one in your meeting will know it's there.

Is this for job seekers?

Absolutely. Your coach helps you organize the search, sharpen your resume, prep for interviews, review how you actually performed, and negotiate the offer. It's just as useful if you're aiming for a promotion or leveling up in your current role.

Will my employer care?

Work Coach doesn't capture proprietary information — it analyzes you, not your company's secrets. Your recordings and transcripts are private and never shared.

Do you share data with anyone?

No. Not your employer. Not other users. Your recordings and transcripts are yours. Data isn't used to train shared models. You can delete everything anytime.

Who is this for?

Anyone who wants to be better in professional conversations — whether you're leading a team, collaborating with peers, or interviewing for your next role.

What does it cost?

Plans start at $100/month for the AI coach, $250/month to add human review, and $500/month for live coaching sessions. Every plan starts with a 2-week free trial — no credit card required.
