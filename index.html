Frequently asked questions

What do I need to get started?

Download the Mac app and record your first meeting. That's it. Work Coach builds your coaching profile over time as it learns your patterns, your goals, and how you show up.

Does a bot join my calls?

No. Work Coach captures audio locally on your Mac. No bot participant, no awkward notification. No one in your meeting will know it's there.

Is this for job seekers?

Absolutely — record practice interviews, catch filler words, role-play tough questions with your AI coach, and hear how you actually sound before the real thing. It's also great for people who want to level up in their current role.

Will my employer care?

Work Coach doesn't capture proprietary information — it analyzes you, not your company's secrets. Your recordings and transcripts are private and never shared.

Do you share data with anyone?

No. Not your employer. Not other users. Your recordings and transcripts are yours. Data isn't used to train shared models. You can delete everything anytime.

Who is this for?

Anyone who wants to be better in professional conversations — whether you're leading a team, collaborating with peers, or interviewing for your next role.
