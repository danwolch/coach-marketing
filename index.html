An affordable executive coach

Work Coach gives you senior-level partnership without the traditional coaching price tag or blind spots.

  • $50 instead of $25,000— Exec coaching can cost $25,000 for a months long engagement with limited touchpoints.
  • Company context included—no need to teach an outsider your org, industry, or product from scratch.
  • Continuous research instead of two meetings a month; the coach proactively sends fresh insights for you.
  • Unlimited context— Coach can review all of your 1:1 transcripts, your whole performance review. More than a human coach could keep up with.

Get a 360 review

It gathers candid input, anonymizes the themes, and turns them into a clear picture of your strengths, blind spots, and next steps.

  • Interview your manager—understand how you are perceived by leadership.
  • Interview your peers—your peers are the most important way to build consensus and influence.
  • Interview your direct reports—understand what your direct reports won't say to your face.

Manage up like a pro.

Show up to every interaction with your manager with a clear game plan that you've thought through and have planned for.

  • Leverage your 1:1 transcripts with your manager.
  • Input your manager's personality type and preferences for the coach to tailor recommendations to.
  • Play out scenarios of situations you will encounter with your manager.

FAQ

How do I give it context fast?

Start with a 5‑minute voice onboarding. Optionally add past reviews/goals later.

Will it replace my meeting note tool?

No—this is for coaching, not notes. You can opt‑in select meetings purely to improve your leadership.

Will it slow me down at work?

Sessions are short, mobile‑friendly, and produce clear actions. No tab‑sprawl.

Who sees my data?

You. Not your employer. You can delete your data anytime.
